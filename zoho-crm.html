Zoho CRM FAQ

Common questions about implementation and automation.

How long does a typical implementation take?

Most projects take 3–6 weeks depending on data volume and the complexity of required scripts. We usually have the core modules ready within 14 days.

Do you handle historical data migration?

Yes. I migrate data from Excel, Salesforce, HubSpot, or secondary Zoho accounts while ensuring data cleanliness and deduplication during the transfer.

Can you connect Zoho CRM to other non-Zoho tools?

Absolutely. If the tool has an API or webhook support, I can connect it to Zoho. Common integrations include Stripe, WordPress, Calendly, and GAds.
